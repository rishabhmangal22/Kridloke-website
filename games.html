Modern Mahabharat

Modern Mahabharat is a 5 vs 5 esports game in which 2 teams of 5 players each fight for gold.
The 2 teams fight in a stadium where gold is being protected by asuras and the two teams have
to defeat the asuras to get gold. The team with the most gold wins. Once one of the player dies
in battle,that player is removed from the match.

Kalki The Vanquisher of Darkness

This is a role playing game based on Kalki Puran. Lord Kalki is the hero of the game and he has to
save the world from Kali, an Evil demon who controles people's minds and make them do self destructive actions.
Kali has mind control abilities and can control the mind of everyone except Kalki,his wife Padmavati and Lord Shiva.
